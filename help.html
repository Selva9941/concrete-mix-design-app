How to use the application (Step by Step)
  1. Login using your email and password.
  2. Open New Mix Design from the sidebar.
  3. Enter design inputs and material costs.
  4. Click Calculate Mix to generate mix quantities and estimated cost.
  5. Click Save Design to keep the design in Saved Designs.
  6. Open Report to print or download as PDF.

About the Application

Concrete Mix Design App is built for civil engineering students and professionals to estimate concrete mix quantities and material cost with an IS 10262:2019 aligned calculation workflow.
