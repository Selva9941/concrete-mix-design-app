Concrete Mix Learning Panel
Understanding Mix Components

A balanced concrete mix needs proper proportioning of cement, fine aggregate, coarse aggregate, water, and admixture. In IS 10262:2019 based design, water-cement ratio and aggregate grading strongly influence strength and durability.

Use the recipe panel to start with recommended ratios, then fine-tune based on workability, exposure condition, and material quality.
